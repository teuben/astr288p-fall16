ASTR 288P: UNIX and Scientific Programming (Fall 2016)

Class Structure

Instructor will guide students in their learning, with problems and assignments being somewhat self-paced. Formal lectures will occur in some sessions, but most time will be taken up by students working on programming assigments. Instructor will be present for guidance and trouble-shooting. Student will have access to the computer lab (CSS 0224) when it is otherwise not in use. Contact instructor if access is needed.
